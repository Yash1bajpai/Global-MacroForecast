MODEL VALIDATION

Accuracy Proof

Rigorous evaluation metrics and data leakage prevention — because trustworthy forecasts demand transparent validation.

How We Prevent Data Leakage

Every model is designed to ensure it cannot cheat — these metrics reflect real-world performance.

  • Temporal Train/Test Split: Strict chronological splitting. Test data is always from a future period never seen during training.
  • No Future Feature Leakage: Lags and rolling stats are strictly past-dated.
  • Recursive Step-by-Step Forecast: The model predicts t+1, feeds it back as lag, and predicts t+2, simulating real deployment.

The Ensemble Approach

We utilize a dynamically weighted ensemble of two distinct architectures:

  • LightGBM (Tree-based): Captures complex non-linear relationships and interactions between macro indicators (e.g., Interest Rates x CPI). Hyperparameters optimized via Optuna.
  • SARIMA (Statistical): Captures the fundamental linear trend and seasonality of the GDP series.
  • Inverse RMSE Weighting: The final forecast dynamically favors the model with the lowest historical Root Mean Square Error.
